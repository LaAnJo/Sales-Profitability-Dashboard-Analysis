# Sales-Profitability-Dashboard-Analysis
Sales & Profitability Dashboard Analysis
Overview

This document summarizes insights derived from an interactive sales dashboard. The analysis focuses on sales performance, profit margins, geographic trends, and product subcategories, with particular attention to Bookcases, Technology products, and Furniture across U.S. states and time periods.

All answers are based on visual evidence from the dashboard, including maps, sparklines, and aggregated tables, with relevant screenshots referenced in the accompanying Word document.

5. Texas Technology Products: Sales Trend Analysis

After filtering the dashboard to Texas and Technology products, the sparklines reveal varied performance across subcategories:

Copiers show strong sales and the highest profit margin, representing a clear growth opportunity.

Accessories and Phones display moderate profit margins with relatively stable sales trends, suggesting steady but incremental growth potential.

Machines exhibit volatile sales and weak profitability, signaling a major concern.

Management Summary

Overall, Technology sales in Texas present promising opportunities, particularly in Copiers. However, Machines require immediate strategic review due to poor profitability and inconsistent performance.

Conclusion

This dashboard analysis highlights:

Strong geographic concentration of sales in states like California

High profitability in niche subcategories such as Labels

Clear growth opportunities in Technology Copiers

Critical concerns around Machines, particularly in Texas

These insights can support data-driven decision-making, helping management prioritize profitable segments while addressing underperforming areas.
